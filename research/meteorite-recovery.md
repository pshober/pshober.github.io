I work on the data-reduction and analysis side of three wide-field fireball networks:
the **Desert Fireball Network** (DFN, during my PhD), the **Global Fireball Observatory**
(GFO), and **FRIPON**, where I have been part of the scientific core team since 2022. That
work runs from multi-station astrometry and photometry through trajectories, orbits and
dark-flight modelling, and includes helping plan and carry out the ground searches that
recover meteorites from well-observed falls.

I also led a systematic comparison of the FRIPON, DFN, WMPL and AMOS reduction pipelines on
a common Geminid sample ([Shober et al. 2026, *A&A*](https://doi.org/10.1051/0004-6361/202554364)),
and have contributed to drone- and machine-learning-assisted search methods, often in
coordination with citizen-science partners such as Vigie-Ciel.

## Recovered meteorites

Falls I have helped recover and/or characterise in the literature:

- **Arpu Kuilpu** (H5), traced to the outer main belt. [Shober et al. 2022, *MAPS*](https://doi.org/10.1111/maps.13813)
- **Madura Cave**, an Australian fall with reconstructed trajectory and orbital history. [Devillepoix, Sansom, Shober et al. 2022, *MAPS*](https://doi.org/10.1111/maps.13820)
- **Winchcombe** (CM2), a rapidly recovered, pristine carbonaceous fall. [King et al. 2022, *Science Advances*](https://doi.org/10.1126/sciadv.abq3925) · [McMullan et al. 2024, *MAPS*](https://doi.org/10.1111/maps.13977)
- **Saint-Pierre-le-Viger**, meteorites dropped over Normandy by asteroid 2023 CX1, one of the few objects detected before impact. [Egal et al. 2025, *Nature Astronomy*](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41550-025-02659-8)
- **Ménétréol**, a rapid recovery with pristine analysis (in review). Anderson, Anghel, Shober et al., *MAPS*
- **Puli Ilkaringuru** (H5), a Nullarbor fall recovered by the Desert Fireball Network. [Meteoritical Bulletin](https://www.lpi.usra.edu/meteor/metbull.php?code=79543)
- **Kybo-Lintos**, the first meteorite recovered using a drone and a machine-learning search. [Anderson et al. 2022, *ApJL*](https://doi.org/10.3847/2041-8213/ac66d4)
